ABC-WDC

ABC College of Engineering and Technology is a prestigious college and is located in Erode. It's club activities assures their brilliant standards and our club for Women's Development is a great example to prove this. It was originally initiated in late 90s, now officially handled by the students currently pursuing the pre-final year. The chairperson is a talented woman who keeps updating herself with latest techs to enrich her students. As the students team consists of young bloods we can expect thrilling experiences to the candidates from the club.
